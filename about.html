Our Core Beliefs

What we stand for and believe in

Our Vision

We exist to connect people to Jesus Christ, and to see people saved, healed, set free, discipled, equipped, empowered and serving.

Our Mission

Our mission is to lead people to become fully devoted followers of Christ, help people find God, grow their faith, discover their purpose and make a difference.

Our Creed

I believe in one God, the father almighty, maker of heaven and earth, of all that is seen and unseen. I believe and trust in Jesus christ, our redeemer who saves us from sin and isolation to be joined together as children of God.

Meet Our Leader

Guided by God's wisdom and direction

Apostle Enoch Ocansey

General Overseer

With over 20 years of ministry experience, Apostle Ocansey has dedicated his life to spreading the Gospel and nurturing believers. His dynamic teaching and prophetic ministry have touched countless lives across Ghana and beyond.

Apostle Ocansey is passionate about seeing people transformed by the power of God's Word and walking in their divine purpose.
